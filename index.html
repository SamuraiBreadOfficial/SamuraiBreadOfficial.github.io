Addons

My addons focus on fantasy and mythical styles. In my addons, you will find ores inspired by Greek mythology, such as orichalc and adamantine. I also love Slavic mythology, so some elements of that may appear in my addons.

I enjoy chill vibes, so sometimes I add custom songs to these addons. Occasionally, I rewrite the music system based on the in-game scoreboard, allowing you to adjust it via simple commands. For example: /scoreboard players set @s music_vol 1 (this sets the music volume to 10%).
