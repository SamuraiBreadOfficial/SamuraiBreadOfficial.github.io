About

Discover the world of Endfallen's Races — a feature-rich add-on that introduces four unique, playable races into Minecraft: Bedrock Edition:

  • Elves – agile beings with enhanced jump height, night vision, and mastery over magic
  • Dwarves – stout miners with superior mining speed and specialized gear for underground survival
  • Felines – fast, stealthy hunters with no fall damage, permanent Speed II, and the powerful Feline Dash
  • Avians – winged humanoids capable of gliding and moving swiftly through the skies

Each race comes with its own custom armor sets, racial abilities, and lore-inspired traits that enhance roleplay and combat alike. From the sleek Feline Stalker Guard to the high-tier Dwarven equipment, your gear reflects your origins.

Central to the experience is the Race Orb — a powerful item that lets players permanently select their race through an intuitive in-game menu. Crafted using a rare Spirit’s Heart (dropped by the elusive Spirit entity) alongside obsidian and sculk, the Race Orb is the gateway to a fully customized adventure.

The add-on also introduces a modular crafting system, centered around the Redstone Component — a vital material used in creating powerful equipment such as the Heavy Hammer and Redstone Component Armor Set. Supporting items like the Reinforced Iron Handle and Heavy Hammer Head make crafting immersive and rewarding.

Music lovers will appreciate the overhauled Music System, featuring the Music Player and exclusive ERA-themed discs. Convert vanilla music discs or use new custom ones like Creator, Precipice, and Saccharine, then play them in-game to add atmosphere to your journeys.

With optimized performance for both mobile and console players, a polished UI, and extensive balancing, Endfallen's Races is more than just a cosmetic pack — it's a full-featured expansion of your Minecraft experience.

Inspired by the spirit of the "Origins Mod," this add-on brings fresh gameplay mechanics, deep character identity, and endless potential for storytelling. Choose your destiny, forge your legend, and dive into the rich world of Endfallen.
